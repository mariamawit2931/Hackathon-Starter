# BizPath AI 🚀

## 💡 Project Idea
BizPath AI is an AI-powered platform that recommends realistic business opportunities based on a user's budget, skills, education level, and location.

It helps aspiring entrepreneurs discover what business they can actually start instead of guessing.

---

## 🎯 Problem
Many people want to start businesses but:
- They don’t know what they can afford
- They don’t know what skills they need
- They don’t know where to start

---

## 💡 Solution
BizPath AI analyzes user input and recommends:
- Suitable business ideas
- Startup cost estimates
- Required skills
- Readiness score ("Can I afford this?")
- Local business suggestions
